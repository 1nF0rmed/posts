Arkency became known for our DDD efforts in the Rails community. DDD together with CQRS and Event Sourcing helped us dealing with large Rails apps. At some point we also started open-source tooling to support introducing DDD in Rails apps. This blogpost aims to highlight where we were, where we are and what is the vision for the future, for the RailsEventStore ecosystem.
